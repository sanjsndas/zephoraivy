Our commitment

At Zephora Ivy Dominion, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, garden events, and newsletters.

Information we collect

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number (only when provided via forms).
  • Garden preferences: zone, plant interests, and consultation history (to improve our recommendations).
  • Usage data: anonymous analytics to understand how our content is enjoyed.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries, send seasonal garden letters, and share event updates.
  • To improve our content, products, and services based on collective feedback.
  • Never sell, rent, or trade your data — we keep it in the soil, not in the market.

Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. Our garden is digital and physical — both are guarded with care.

🌱 Your trust is our root system. You can request deletion or correction anytime.

Third-party links

Our site may contain links to external resources (podcasts, books, seed suppliers). We are not responsible for their privacy practices — we encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page occasionally to reflect new practices. The latest version will always be posted here. Last updated: April 2026.
