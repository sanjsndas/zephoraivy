The Dominion story

Founded in 2018 by a collective of horticulturists and ecologists, Zephora Ivy Dominion began as a small research plot in the foothills. Today, we curate a global network of trial gardens, seed exchanges, and educational content — all rooted in the belief that gardening is an act of radical hope.

Our name honors the ivy that climbs through ruins and the zephyr winds that carry pollen across continents. We are gardeners, storytellers, and stewards.
