    Frequently asked questions

    What is the best time to start a vegetable garden?

    After the last frost date. For most temperate zones, that’s mid-spring. Use our zone lookup tool.

    How often should I water raised beds?

    Deeply and less frequently — aim for 1 inch per week, adjusting for rainfall and mulch.

    Can I grow perennials in containers?

    Yes, with appropriate winter protection and large pots. Choose dwarf varieties.
